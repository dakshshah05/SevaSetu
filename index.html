Our Mission

SevaSetu is a localized civic collaboration ecosystem. We empower restaurants to instantly route excess food to active volunteers, facilitate community-driven sanitation campaigns (Swachh Bharat Abhiyan), and provide a rewards currency sponsored by corporate partners (Zepto, Swiggy, and local merchants) to keep volunteers motivated.

For Restaurants

Upload unsold/surplus food. Avoid wastage, earn Seva Rating points, and receive tax exemptions.
